
The next phase after implementing the executive KPIs should focus on customer analysis rather than jumping directly into Power BI development.

This stage aims to answer business questions such as:

* Who are the company's top customers?
* What percentage of total revenue do they generate?
* Is there an 80/20 revenue concentration (Pareto Principle)?
* Which customer categories contribute the most to sales?

The objective is to move beyond reporting metrics and begin generating meaningful business insights. These findings will later support the design of a more effective and insightful Power BI dashboard.

At this point, the project transitions from measuring performance to understanding the drivers behind that performance. This is where the most valuable analytical insights often emerge.
